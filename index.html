  • 5-Ingredient Tomato Soup

    This soup is so easy to make, it's perfect for a quick and healthy weeknight meal. Simply combine canned tomatoes, vegetable broth, garlic, basil, and salt in a pot and bring to a simmer. Then, use an immersion blender or regular blender to puree the soup until smooth. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil leaves, or a dollop of sour cream.

  • 3-Ingredient Baked Goat Cheese Dip

    This dip is a crowd-pleaser that is sure to be a hit at your next party or gathering. Simply combine goat cheese, honey, and lavender oil in a small baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10-15 minutes, or until the goat cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve with your favorite crackers, bread, or vegetables.

  • Broccoli Alfredo Pasta

    This pasta dish is a creamy and flavorful vegetarian option that is ready in just 30 minutes. Simply cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add broccoli florets and cook until tender-crisp. Stir in Alfredo sauce, Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper to taste. Toss with cooked pasta and serve immediately.

  • Easy Sheet Pan Roasted Greek Salmon and Broccoli

    This dish is a healthy and flavorful one-pan meal that is perfect for a busy weeknight. Simply preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Toss salmon fillets and broccoli florets with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper. Spread salmon and broccoli on a baking sheet and roast for 15-20 minutes, or until salmon is cooked through and broccoli is tender.

  • One Pot Spinach Mushroom Pasta

    This pasta dish is a quick and easy vegetarian option that is packed with flavor. Simply heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened. Stir in spinach and mushrooms and cook until wilted. Add pasta, vegetable broth, and Parmesan cheese. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until pasta is cooked through. Serve immediately.
