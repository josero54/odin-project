5-Ingredient Tomato Soup

Weeknight meals can often feel like a chore. Between work, errands, and activities, finding
the time and energy to cook a healthy and satisfying meal can be a challenge. That's
where this 5-ingredient tomato soup comes in. This soup is incredibly easy to make, requiring
minimal prep and cooking time. It's also packed with flavor and nutrients, making it a perfect
choice for a busy weeknight.

The key to this soup's simplicity is using canned tomatoes. Canned tomatoes are a lifesaver in the
kitchen, offering a delicious and convenient way to incorporate tomatoes into your dishes. For this
soup, you'll want to use high-quality canned tomatoes, such as San Marzano tomatoes. These tomatoes
have a richer flavor than your average canned tomato, which will elevate the overall taste of the soup.

In addition to canned tomatoes, this soup requires just a handful of other ingredients: vegetable broth,
garlic, basil, and salt. The vegetable broth adds depth and body to the soup, while the garlic and
basil provide aromatic and herbal notes. A pinch of salt helps to bring out the natural flavors of the
ingredients.

To make this soup, simply combine all of the ingredients in a pot and bring to a simmer. Once the soup
has simmered for about 15 minutes, use an immersion blender or regular blender to puree the soup
until smooth. If you prefer a chunky soup, you can leave some of the tomatoes unblended. Serve the
soup hot with your favorite toppings, such as grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil leaves, or a dollop
of sour cream.
