Broccoli Alfredo Pasta

If you're looking for a vegetarian pasta dish that is both creamy and flavorful,
look no further than broccoli alfredo pasta. This dish is made with fresh broccoli, a
creamy alfredo sauce, and Parmesan cheese. It's ready in just 30 minutes, making it a
perfect choice for a quick and easy weeknight meal.

The key to this dish is the alfredo sauce. You can use a store-bought alfredo sauce for
convenience, but making your own alfredo sauce is surprisingly easy and yields far superior
results. To make your own alfredo sauce, you'll simply need butter, heavy cream,
Parmesan cheese, garlic, and nutmeg. The butter and heavy cream create a rich and creamy base,
while the Parmesan cheese adds a salty and nutty flavor. The garlic and nutmeg provide warmth and
depth of flavor.

The broccoli in this dish adds a pop of color, freshness, and nutrients. For the best results,
use fresh broccoli florets that are crisp and tender. You can steam, roast, or blanch the
broccoli according to your preference.
