3-Ingredient Baked Goat Cheese Dip

Looking for a quick and easy appetizer that is sure to impress your guests? Look no
further than this 3-ingredient baked goat cheese dip. This dip is incredibly simple
to make, requiring just three ingredients: goat cheese, honey, and lavender oil. Yet,
it has a complex and flavorful profile that will have your guests raving.
The star of this dip is, of course, the goat cheese. Goat cheese has a tangy and
creamy flavor that pairs beautifully with the sweetness of honey. For this recipe,
you'll want to use a good quality goat cheese, such as French ch√®vre. Be sure to soften
the goat cheese to room temperature before baking,
as this will help it to melt evenly and create a smooth dip.

The honey in this recipe helps to balance out
the tanginess of the goat cheese and adds a touch of sweetness. You can use any type of
honey that you like, but for a more floral flavor, consider using lavender honey.
Lavender honey is made from the nectar of lavender flowers and has a subtle floral
aroma that complements the goat cheese perfectly.

The final ingredient in this recipe is lavender oil. Lavender oil adds a touch of floral perfume
to the dip and elevates its flavor profile. However, it's important to use pure lavender oil
that is intended for culinary purposes. Do not use essential oils, as they are not safe for
consumption.

To make this dip, simply combine the goat cheese, honey, and lavender oil in a small baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10-15 minutes, or until the goat cheese is melted and bubbly.
Serve the dip hot with your favorite crackers, bread, or vegetables.
