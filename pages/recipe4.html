Easy Sheet Pan Roasted Greek Salmon and Broccoli

Imagine a delicious and healthy dinner that practically cooks itself. That's the beauty of this easy sheet pan roasted Greek salmon and broccoli recipe. This dish requires minimal prep work and cleanup, making it a perfect choice for busy weeknights or when you're simply looking for a hassle-free meal.

The key to this recipe is using a sheet pan. Sheet pans are incredibly versatile kitchen tools that allow you to roast a variety of ingredients at once. This not only saves time but also helps to create evenly cooked and flavorful food.

For the Greek twist, this recipe uses a simple marinade made with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, and salt. The olive oil helps to keep the salmon moist, while the lemon juice adds a touch of acidity that brightens the flavor. Garlic and oregano provide savory and herbaceous notes, and salt enhances the overall taste.

To prepare this dish, simply toss the salmon fillets and broccoli florets with the Greek marinade. Spread them out on a baking sheet and roast in a preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the broccoli is tender-crisp. This hands-off approach allows you to relax while the oven does all the work.

Once cooked, the salmon and broccoli will be beautifully caramelized and bursting with flavor. Serve this dish with a side of rice, quinoa, or couscous for a complete and satisfying meal.
